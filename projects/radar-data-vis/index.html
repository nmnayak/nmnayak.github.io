My Society

A complete solution for your housing complex.

Free

Screenshots

The latest feature of this app is the "My Society Security System". This security system has been designed specifically keeping in mind the requirements of housing societies, and includes:
- An easy-to-use interface for security personnel.
- An alert system which allows you to accept or deny entry to visitors.
- Allows the security personnel to contact you via phone calls if you do not respond to the alerts on your smartphone.
- Provides you with the details of the visitor.

For the Society Residents:
• Simple & User-friendly interface.
• Society Notices at your fingertips: Now never check the noticeboard ever again! All notifications just a tap away.
• Have a request or complaint? Too lazy to write to the society office?
Through this app, you can conveniently send a complaint or request directly to the society administration.
• Request Forms for renovation, car parking, and flat renting available as PDF downloads right on the app.
• Love reading? If you want to check if the society library has the book you want, just head over to the society library section and start searching!
• Now access the society's essential contacts right on the app and search for the shop/organisation/clinic etc. you want to call.
• Now never miss a single bill! Get a reminder to pay your society maintenance bill every month.
• Prepare for festivities well in advance and be notified by the app about notices and upcoming events in your housing society.
• Share your society's location directly through this app.

For the Society Administrator:
• Absolutely free of cost!
• Easily register your housing society.
• Simple accept and reject system to prevent fake users from getting society data.
• Stop wastage of paper and reduce your expenses & manual efforts! Post updates on the app instead of sending printouts to each and every apartment.
• Easily add new notices, events, and reminders along with their date and time. In case of cancellation, an event can simply be deleted by long pressing on the event.
• View complaints and requests of the residents listed according to their flat number and long press on the complaint to mark it as resolved.
