Real-time Bus Tracking System

Public transport has never been so convenient.

Free

Screenshots

This solution aims to make public bus transport more convenient for people.

According to the timetable, the bus I take to go to school has a frequency of 30 minutes, but it is late most of the times due to heavy traffic. This gave me the idea of creating the app.

After the bus conductor or driver installs the app, they can login with their account details, select their route and start bus tracking.

On the user side, after a person installs the app, he can enter the bus number he wants to travel in. If he does not know his bus number, he can enter source and destination stop and then select the bus. After selecting the bus, he can view the bus stops and track the live location of the bus.

Using this app, you can check the current location of your bus and plan your travel accordingly.
